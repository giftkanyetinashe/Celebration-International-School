Welcome to the Dynamic Leadership Team at Celebration International School!

At Celebration International School, we take pride in our extraordinary team of leaders who are trailblazers in education and catalysts for success. Our leadership team comprises visionaries who bring boundless expertise, unwavering dedication, and an infectious passion to their roles.

Meet Our Inspiring Leaders:

Ms Lorna Jackson – Principal Infant School

I have been in education 43 years, teaching grade two. I am passionate about sharing the gospel with students, as well as empowering parents to "parent with purpose". I love sports and creative activities.

Melanie du Toit – Principal Elementary School

With over three decades of experience as an educator, my focus lies in classroom management and curriculum development. My passion for Christian education drives me to create an environment where students thrive academically and spiritually. By fostering inclusive relationships with students, parents, and colleagues, I aim to build a collaborative and supportive educational community. Guided by a belief in the inherent worth of young individuals, my mission is to instil compassion, integrity, and servant leadership shaping responsible citizens and ambassadors for the Kingdom and the marketplace.

Mrs Munyaradzi,s Bio – Principal High school

In my 23 years of teaching, God has made it clear to me  that education is a media to one's destiny. It is through education, formal and informal, that the children that are entrusted to us answer the critical life questions, Who am ? and  Why am I here?. Celebration International School not only answers to academic excellence but also shapes the learners' call to serve as they discover what God called them to do. I am privileged to be a member of the team that transforms lives and reforms nations.
