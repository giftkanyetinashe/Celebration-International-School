Sukkot / Feast of Tabernacles

Sukkot, commonly translated as Feast of Tabernacles or Feast of the Ingathering, is a Jewish Biblical holiday. It is the remembering of God’s provision, protection, and presence during the 40 years in the wilderness. We celebrated this feast with song and dance and a word from Pastor Bonnie. At the end of the day, we donated the fruit and vegetables – that you, the parents, so freely gave – to Hatcliffe Church. Thank you to everyone for making the Feast of Tabernacles a significant blessing.
